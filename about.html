About the Charleston Shrimp & Grits Event

A Lowcountry tradition celebrating food, chefs, and community

What Is the Shrimp & Grits Charleston Event?

The Charleston Shrimp & Grits event is an annual culinary celebration honoring one of the Lowcountry’s most iconic dishes. Each year, local chefs, restaurants, and culinary teams gather to showcase their unique take on shrimp & grits — blending tradition, creativity, and Southern flavor.

Guests enjoy tasting a wide range of shrimp & grits styles, from classic Lowcountry preparations to bold, modern interpretations, all while enjoying music, drinks, and Charleston hospitality.

A Celebration of Charleston Food Culture

Shrimp & grits has deep roots in South Carolina history, originating as a simple fisherman’s breakfast and evolving into a signature dish found on menus throughout Charleston. This event was created to celebrate that evolution — highlighting both heritage and innovation.

Beyond the food, the event brings together chefs, locals, visitors, and sponsors who share a passion for Charleston’s culinary scene and community spirit.

What to Expect at the Event

  • Chef-prepared shrimp & grits tasting stations
  • People’s Choice and judge-selected awards
  • Live music and entertainment
  • Local drinks, vendors, and sponsors
  • Opportunities to meet local chefs
  • Indoor and outdoor event spaces
  • Community-driven atmosphere
  • A fun experience for locals and visitors alike

Chefs & Competition

Each year features a rotating lineup of Charleston chefs and restaurant teams. Some events focus on friendly competition, while others emphasize collaboration and celebration. Past participants have included chefs from well-known Charleston restaurants as well as rising local talent.

Awards are typically based on guest voting and judge feedback, recognizing creativity, flavor, and presentation.

Sponsors & Community Partners

The Shrimp & Grits Charleston event is made possible through the support of local sponsors, including restaurants, beverage brands, seafood providers, and community partners. Sponsorship opportunities help support event production while connecting brands with Charleston’s vibrant food scene.

Interested in becoming a sponsor or participating as a chef? Opportunities vary each year and are announced leading up to the event.

When & Where

The event is typically held once per year at a Charleston-area venue, with dates and locations announced in advance. Venues often include outdoor spaces, waterfront locations, or popular community gathering spots.

Be sure to check the website regularly for updated event details, ticket information, and announcements.
